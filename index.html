FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions our clients ask before starting their application journey.

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 5–15 business days, while study and work visas can take 4–12 weeks. We always keep you informed of expected timelines and any embassy delays.
Most applications require a valid international passport, recent passport photographs, bank statements (3–6 months), proof of employment or enrollment, accommodation details, and a return flight itinerary. The exact requirements depend on your destination. Book a consultation and we will give you a personalized checklist.
No legitimate agency can guarantee visa approval, as the final decision rests with the embassy. However, with a 98% success rate built over 8+ years, our expertise in preparing strong, complete applications gives our clients the best possible chance of success.
Our refund policy depends on the service package chosen. Embassy fees are non-refundable by embassies. Our service fees may be partially refunded in specific circumstances. We discuss this transparently during your initial consultation so you have full clarity before proceeding.
We accept bank transfers, POS payments at our Ikeja office, and online payment options. Full payment details are provided after your initial consultation and upon agreement of the service package. We do not request payment through unofficial channels.
