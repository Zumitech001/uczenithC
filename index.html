Who We Are

Precision Marketing Meets Maximum Reach

"Speak to our talented team about structuring a campaign perfectly suited to your budget and media strategy."

UCZenith Concept is a specialized outdoor advertising company with over 15 years' expertise in the industry. A subsidiary of Proview Advertising Limited, founded by Theophilus Isibor in 2008, we have grown from a handful of billboards into one of Nigeria's well-established outdoor advertising businesses.

Our extensive inventory comprises over 50 premium advertising sites strategically located across Lagos and Abuja. Renowned for expert insights, tactical abilities, and personalized media relationships, we provide the perfect balance of targeted sites and extensive exposure to meet all your outdoor marketing needs.

Our Story

Founded in 2008.
Built on Results.

UCZenith Concept is a subsidiary of Proview Advertising Limited, founded by Theophilus Isibor — a product of ESTAM University, Portnovo, Benin Republic, with degrees in HR and Public Administration.

Beginning with a small base of loyal clients and just a handful of billboards, the company has grown into a well-established business through innovative site selection, tactical execution, and skillful marketing. We maintain 4 permanent staff and over 30 contract staff on-site, handling construction and fabrication projects.

"Site selection, tactical execution, and skillful marketing — the pillars of our 15-year success."

In response to Nigeria's growing energy needs, UCZenith has diversified into Solar Energy Systems since 2022 — making waves across the industry with reliable, clean energy solutions.
